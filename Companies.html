Lamborghini

Italian

Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, this Italian marque revolutionized supercars with the 1966 Miura, the world’s first mid-engine V12 sports car. 

Fun fact: Ferruccio started the company after Enzo Ferrari dismissed his complaints about clutch issues in Ferraris, prompting him to build a better car himself! 

Ferrari

Italian

Established in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari, the iconic Prancing Horse brand gave us legends like the 250 GTO and the F40.

Fun fact: Ferrari’s factory-built race cars for other brands before making road cars, and the F40 (1987) was the last model approved by Enzo himself. 

Ferrari

Italian

Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, this French brand redefined luxury with the 1930 Type 41 Royale and modern speed with the 1,001 HP Veyron (2005).

Fun fact: The Royale’s engine was so massive (12.7L) it powered WWII fighter planes, and only six were ever made.
